About Us

Company History

Tata Steel Limited, an Indian multinational steel-making company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, and based in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, is a prominent member of the Tata Group. Formerly known as Tata Iron and Steel Company Limited (TISCO), it stands as one of the world's largest steel producers, boasting an annual crude steel capacity of 35 million tonnes. Renowned for its global reach, Tata Steel operates across diverse regions and holds a significant commercial presence internationally.


In the financial year ending on March 31, 2023, the group, excluding operations in Southeast Asia, achieved a consolidated turnover of US$31 billion. Notably, Tata Steel holds the position of the steel company in India, measured by domestic production, with an impressive annual capacity of 21.6 million tonnes, trailing only behind Steel Authority of India Ltd. (SAIL). Among Indian steel enterprises, Tata Steel, along with SAIL and Jindal Steel and Power, distinguishes itself as one of the exclusive trio possessing captive iron-ore mines. This strategic advantage contributes to their competitive edge in terms of pricing.
